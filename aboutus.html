OUR COMPANY

Agrifarm Tanzania is a company that links smallholder producers/ local farmers to national and international markets so as to meet food demand. Through us we assist buyer trading and sourcing of consolidated agriculture commodities from multiple farmers or commodity owners

Agribusiness is a fast growing, yet the true potential is stymied by limited mechanization, fragmented markets, price controls and poor infrastructure. MISSION AND VISION

Mission:Agrifarm Tanzania aims to help in connecting of farmers, merchants and plant breeders to the food industry both locally and international by seeking out markets.

Vision:Agrifarm Tanzania focuses on bringing the best value of Tanzania agriculture product to the world by providing high quality products needed and accepted in the world market.

FUNDERMENTAL PURPOSE OF THE COMPANY

The fundamental purpose of Agrifarm Tanzania is to create and supply agriculture products for end consumption. Agriculture products are naturally produced resources for human consumption or other uses.

We focus on distributing quality product to customers with quality assurance. To create awareness on agriculture product found in Tanzania through social media groups, attending of various meetings done all around the globe.

OUR PRIDE

At Agrifarm Tanzania, we are so proud of helping local farmers and ensuring high-quality products.Our company works to connect farmers, merchants, and plant breeders in a smooth and efficient way. We believe in supporting farmers by giving them knowledge they need to grow crops well. through this, we help them produce better food for everyone. Our focus on quality means the products we bring to the market are always fresh and healthy.
